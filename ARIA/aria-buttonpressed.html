aria-pressed on role="button"

Button not pressed

Tested on:

  • iOS 9.31 and VoiceOver
    • Passed: Works as expected. When the button is not pressed, iOS announces "not pressed". When the button is pressed, iOS announces "checked" and status changes to "pressed".
  • iOS 8.4 and VoiceOver
    • Failed: Button is announced as checkbox without the status. When button is pressed, the status doesn't change. With iOS 8.x or lower versions, it is not usable.
